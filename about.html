About Us

Sands of Time is a community of ambitious and dedicated sand lovers from all over the world. Established in 1942, we have been supplying sand from all around the globe to our customers. We have it all from Hawaii to the beaches of Melbourne. The sand is by pounds from Blue Japanese Sand to Coral Sand. All from the comfort in your own home. We are Bay Area based and take greate pride of the rich sand located near the Pacific Coastline.


Our Mission is to deliver the absolute best of sand to you. We have given 100 percent satisfaction to our customers. We reveive nothing but the best about our products. We can assure you that you'll be happy with any purchase of ours!
