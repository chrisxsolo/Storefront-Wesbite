Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the status of my order?

Send us an email with your order number and we'll let you know.

Can I return my sand?

You cannot. All the sand sold at Sands of Time comes with a quality guarantee, which is not possible if we accepted returns.

Is your sand edible?

No sand sold at Sands of Time is safe for consumption, including the Sandbox Sand.

Are there payment installment plans for any kind of sand?

We do not currently offer payment installment plans at this time.

Do you ship internationally?

We support shipping to anywhere in the United States, Canada, Mexico, the European Union, and the United Kingdom. For inquiries about other regions, please contact us directly.
